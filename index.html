Guide to make your own Vegetable Bud.
WARNING: This method is bad for your lungs, (+18)

  1. Pick your vegetable that you'd like to use for the bud. (ex. carrot)
  2. Take your vegetable and use a kitchen knife or any kind of blade to cut the carrot in small pieces, (ex. 2cm by 2cm).
  3. Take any sort of cigarette that contain tobacco and tear it into equal parts, ensure that the tobacco remains inside the cigarette and that it doesn't spread everywhere.
  4. Place the tobacco onto your cigarette paper.
  5. Take your vegetable pieces and place them into the tobacco, make sure that there's 1cm space between every space of the 2cm by 2cm vegetable piece.
  6. Take your cigarette paper and roll it. Using a cigarette roller is easier to accurately roll your bud.
  7. Light up that bad boy and enjoy your new experience of consuming vegetables by means of smoke.
